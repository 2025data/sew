From Kindle:
  1. Draw on draw.html
  2. Click "JSON" button to open the JSON modal
  3. Click "Copy to Clipboard" button
  4. Come to this page
  5. Click "Paste from Clipboard" button above
  6. If that doesn't work, tap in text area and look for Kindle's paste option
  7. Click "Save to Server"

Alternative: If pasting doesn't work, you can connect Kindle via USB, copy the files from the documents folder, and place them in the SewCustom folder on your PC.
